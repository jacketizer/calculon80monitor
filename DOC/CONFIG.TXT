Available Attributes
--------------------

The following attributes should be available in the CONFIG.TXT file:

  * DRV_MEM (MEM)
  * DRV_ISR (ISR)
  * DRV_SIO (COM)
  * DRV_PIO (PIO)
  * DRV_CTC (GPT)

  * DEV_TTY (COM:0)
  * DEV_STM (GPT:0)

  * TTY_BUFFER_SIZE

Example
-------

DRV_MEM=MEM.SYS
DRV_ISR=ISR.SYS
DRV_SIO=SIO_Z80SIO.SYS
DRV_PIO=PIO_Z80PIO.SYS
DRV_GPT=GPT_Z80CTC.SYS

DEV_TTY=SIO:0
DEV_STM=GPT:0

TTY_BUFFER_SIZE=512
